Team 2374 is an extracurricular orginization at Jesuit High School of Portland Oregon. We, the Crusaderbots, compete in the annual Bunny Bots comptetion hosted by Catlin Gabel, and the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). The team was founded in 2007 by alumnus Nik Bowen, and former physics teacher Dr. Catherine Molloseau. The team hovers around approximately 30 aspiring individuals, driven by our motto, "A Posse ad Esse" The team is currently under the direction of Mr. Doug Naimo and Mr. Dan Calkins.

Team 2374 meets 2 to 5 hours per week for during the fall club and preperation season. Hours extend to 8 to 15 hours during the packed winter build season. The team now works out of the Elloriaga Center for Math and Science. We also keep busy during the rest of the year with our outreach, independent projects, our T-Shirt cannon, and Girl's Generation.
